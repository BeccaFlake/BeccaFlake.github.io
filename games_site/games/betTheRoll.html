The first player will player will make a bet on the roll before it occurs. The second player (computer) will then choose if they will match the bet, fold, or raise the bet. If the second player raises the bet, the first player must decide to match, fold, or raise. If the player raises the process repeats.

If a player folds, their current bet goes to the other player, who wins that round.

Each player will roll two 6 sided die to get a total. The player with a higher roll wins that round. The game will end when a player gets $100 or the player chooses to end the game.
