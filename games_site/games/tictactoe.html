Tic Tac Toe is an excellent way to pass long hours by a fire or while waiting for something interesting to happen.

This game is for two players. One player is green (X) and the other is purple (O). The goal is to get three boxes in a row (across, down, or diagonal). When you enter your letter into the box and click 'OK', the box will change to your color. The game ends when a player has three in a row or all of the boxes are filled.
