Fortune Teller

You find yourself seated inside a dimly lit tent, full of knick knacks and baubles from different regions. Across from you sits a woman, draped in colorful fabrics and partially in shadow. She tells you her name is Mitra, and the sign outside the tent says she is a seer. Mitra offers to tell you your fortune, giving you the option of a large fortune or a small fortune.
