Hello there!

Hall of Amusements' games all run on JavaScript and were coded by hand. That means that if you have JavaScript disabled in your browser, they unfortunately will not work. Most of them have a classic fantasy roll playing game (RPG) theme, and all of the games can be played by adults and children alike. A few of the games can be played by more than one person.

Your questions and comments sent through the contact page are read by a live person, pictured on the right. I'm a programming student and a huge fan of video games and table top RPGS. Combining the two into simple little browser games is great fun.
