Hall of Amusements offers a variety of games for all ages and more are added all the time. You can play our games any time you want for free. Games range from Tic Tac Toe to a Rock-Paper-Scissors style battle game. Because of the way Chrome handles some JS actions, the games work best in Firefox.

More information can be found on the About page. If you want to make an account to track your game usage feel free! We are always looking for new ideas, so feel free to use the Contact form to send us your thoughts!
